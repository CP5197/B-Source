User-Specific Chatter Configuration

Configure chatter position, width, and collapsed behavior from each user's settings to match their preferred way of working.

Side Chatter Toggle

Use the side chatter toggle directly from the record view to open the side panel whenever you need access to messages and activities.

Side Chatter View

After clicking the toggle, the side chatter opens alongside the form so you can follow conversations and activities without leaving the record.

Resizable Side Chatter

Resize the side chatter panel by dragging the divider, so users can increase or reduce chatter width based on the amount of form space they need.

Bottom Chatter Configuration

Configure the bottom chatter position from user settings so each user can choose a full-width layout that fits their workflow and screen preference.

Bottom Chatter View

After enabling the bottom layout, the chatter appears below the form in a full-width view that is comfortable for wider screens and long discussion threads.

Collapse Bottom Chatter

Hide the bottom chatter when you need more vertical space for the form, and bring it back only when messages or activities need attention.
